CHANGING GEARS: WEB TO MOBILE

Strategies for designing, developing and managing new age mobile solutions

We are at a juncture where we don't need to question if Mobile is important or not. We know it is!
Having a mobile presence means that businesses are available on the fingertips of their customers, and that they can reach out to new customers faster in addition to engaging with the existing ones more effectively. In order to ensure our clients' success, we have to break open our shackle of old time development and start using disruptive ways of development. To learn and share more about these strategies, we bring to you Converge - Sept,2015 which focusses on strategies around designing, developing and managing new age mobile solutions.

If you have similar interesting experiences in mobile space which you would like to share, we welcome you to PRESENT them in this forum.

As a PARTICIPANT, you will connect with like-minded peers and gain insight from industry practitioners.

If you have any questions/suggestions write to us at converge@thoughtworks.com.
